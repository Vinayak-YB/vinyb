There is always something new and good out there — new films, new music, new places, new ideas. Anthony Bourdain said it best: travel, eat, read, experience as much as you can. That is the approach. 

---

## Cinema

I watch everything — old Hollywood, Bollywood, Kannada cinema, anime, series, documentaries, world cinema. No real filter on genre or era.

What I am drawn to is craft — how a story is written, how a scene is constructed, what a director is actually trying to say. Good writing, good direction, good acting. That is what makes something stick.

Always looking for the next thing worth watching.

---

## Games

Prince of Persia, COD, Assasins Creed growing up. Recently — Uncharted, Ghost of Tsushima, Red Dead Redemption 2. All three are as much about atmosphere and storytelling as they are about gameplay. Ghost of Tsushima especially — the art direction is something else.

---

## Music

Varied is an understatement. Michael Jackson, Charles Bradley, Caught a Ghost, Vampire Weekend on one end. Sonu Nigam, Lucky Ali, Shankar Mahadevan, Bombay Vikings on the other. 

---

## Other

Hiking, gym, chess. Quizzing casually — the kind where you just enjoy knowing things. Reading, slowly building the habit.

---

Classical Carnatic music, the literature, the sensibility — it stays with you no matter where you end up.*
